Intro

A memorable moment from my middle school graduation.

Hello there! I go by UN7X (pronounced 'Unix') or Bridge. I'm a 16 year old teenager with a passion for technology and a knack for details sometimes (some might say I'm a bit of a perfectionist.)

I'm a Mexican-American currently residing in Mexico, having previously lived in the USA. I'm fluent in both English and Spanish, and I'm currently learning Indonesian. When I'm not programming or learning languages, you can often find me exploring worlds in Minecraft or enjoying games on Roblox.

By the way, check out my awesome work.
